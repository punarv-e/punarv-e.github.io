The Punarv-E Mission

The name "Punarv" translates to renewal and restoration. At Punarv-E, we transform the growing electronic waste crisis into an urban mining opportunity. Fully compliant with the stringent E-Waste (Management) Rules, 2022, our state-of-the-art facility bridges the gap between Bangalore's massive IT waste streams and authorized, formal recycling [1].

Our Integrated Services

Corporate ITAD & Sourcing

Direct B2B collection contracts with tech parks and OEMs in the Bangalore corridor, bypassing informal aggregators to secure high-yield material.

Certified Data Destruction

Guaranteed enterprise-grade hardware and software data wiping. We ensure absolute privacy for your corporate assets before recycling.

Refurbishing & Resale

Testing and repairing functional electronics to introduce them back into the secondary market, extending their lifecycle and promoting circularity.

Urban Mining & Extraction

Mechanical shredding and advanced segregation to safely harvest high-value commodities (Gold, Silver, Copper, Aluminum) from populated PCBs.

EPR Certificate Trading

Helping electronics producers meet their legal Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) targets through the generation and secure trading of compliance certificates [1].

Zero-Landfill Processing

Strict KSPCB compliance. Hazardous materials are securely routed to industrial smelters and TSDFs, ensuring zero environmental leakage.
