The Punarv-E Mission

The name "Punarv" translates to renewal and restoration. At Punarv-E Resource Recovery Pvt. Ltd., we transform the growing electronic waste crisis into an urban mining opportunity. Fully compliant with the stringent E-Waste (Management) Rules, 2022, our state-of-the-art facility bridges the gap between Bangalore's massive IT waste streams and authorized, formal recycling[cite: 1].

Our Integrated Services

Corporate ITAD & Sourcing

Certified Data Destruction
